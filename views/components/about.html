About my Dream

La Viola de Papá is a dream that arose some years ago in Málaga, in southern Spain. I decided to surprise my father building a handmade viola specially for him. Putting all my creativity on it, I called it: “La Viola de Papa”. After this experience, I realized that I wanted to learn more about the traditional craft of luthier, so, I did my best with the aim of complete my knowledge and skills in the well-known “Newark School of Violin Making”, located in Newark on Trent. I did, and now, I would like to share my creations with you and if possible, make your dream come true too.
